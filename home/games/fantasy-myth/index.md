# 🪄 Fantasy & Myth

Legendary creatures, magical realms, and mythological epics.

### ❤️ Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 (2025)
A rare masterpiece. It hooks you right from the intriguing prologue with an air of melancholy throughout. Every part of the game feels crafted with love. My GOTY.

### ❤️ Darksiders 2 (2021)
Had a blast playing this. Lovely world design, fun puzzles, and enough variety to keep things fresh.

### Immortals Fenyx Rising (2022)
A thoroughly enjoyable adventure from Ubisoft. Humorous cast, lovely open world that is a joy to glide over.

### Bramble: The Mountain King (2023)
Atmosphere is creepy and beautiful. Based around dark fairy tales with unique boss fights.

### The Witcher series (2021)
Played through the legacy titles. Things getting interesting with the Witcher assembling a rag-tag party to search for Ciri.

### Indika (2026)
A short game following a nun who questions her calling. Increasingly bizarre and ends quite well.
