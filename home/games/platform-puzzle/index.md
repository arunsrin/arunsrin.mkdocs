# :material-puzzle:{ .anim-heart } Platformers & Puzzles

Timing, logic, and creative problem-solving.

### ❤️ Rayman Legends (2019)
Exquisite level design and music. My kids love this. Easy and hard as you want it to be.

### ❤️ INSIDE (2019)
Lovely little gem of a game.

### Cocoon (2023)
Worlds within worlds within worlds. Thoroughly satisfying when everything clicks together.

### Planet of Lana (2023)
Short and sweet with lovely visuals and simple puzzles. No cutscenes or exposition needed.

### A Short Hike (2023)
A short and sweet journey. Feels good to explore the lovely little world.

### Contrast (2022)
Unique mechanism where you can turn into a shadow and move across surfaces.

### Trek to Yomi (2022)
Worth playing just for the visuals. Designed to look like an old Japanese movie.

### The Gunk (2021)
Lovely world design and visuals. Quite relaxing.
