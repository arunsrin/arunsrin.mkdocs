# :material-target:{ .anim-rotate } Shooters (FPS)

Fast-paced action and military epics.

### ❤️ Doom (2016) (2023)
Pure, unadulterated fun. Waves of enemies keep the pressure up and the weapons are immensely satisfying. Aggressive and fast.

### ❤️ Titanfall 2 (2019)
Absolutely phenomenal single-player campaign. One of the few games I've gone back to and replayed. Movement is just *sublime*.

### ❤️ Halo: Reach (2021) & Halo: Infinite (2022)
Reach blew me away both gameplay and story-wise. Infinite's move to open-world and the grappleshot are huge improvements.

### ❤️ Gears of War (Series) (2020)
Mindless fun. Finished the entire legacy series. Phenomenal weapons like the Hammer of Dawn and Lancer.

### Prodeus (2023)
An affectionate tribute to early FPSs like Doom. Very pretty to look at and a blast to play.

### The Callisto Protocol & Dead Space (2023)
Dead Space is a flawless remake. Callisto is very pretty to look at and just the right length.

### High on Life (2022)
Crude, funny and a breath of fresh air. Filled with fun easter eggs and humor.

### Rage 2 (2020)
Loved the post-apocalyptic world-design.

### Call of Juarez: Gunslinger (2023)
A well-made, humorous game in a lovely Western setting. Sniping from afar is a blast.
