# 🎮 Games played in 2026

## Anno: Mutationem

So.. I've taken a liking for the whole cyberpunk / pixel-art aesthetic. This is a 2.5d side scroller with that lovely
art style. On easy mode the game was a solid mix of combat and exploration. The story is decent as well; one of the 
characters is a bit cringey.. the remaining ones make up for it.

## Indika

A short game that I was able to finish in a single day. A lot of walking and some light puzzles. Towards the end it got 
increasingly bizarre, and it ended quite well. The story follows a nun who questions her choices and her ability to do
justice to her calling.
