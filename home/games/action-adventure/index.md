# 🤺 Action & Adventure

Exploration, stealth, and high-octane action.

### ❤️ Assassin's Creed Odyssey (2020)
I've loved Greek mythology all my life so this was an amazing experience. Very nice variety in play styles (ranger/warrior/assassin), and constant loots and upgrades made for an addictive gameplay loop. I've spent ~150 hours on this including the DLCs (*Legacy of the First Blade* and *The Fate of Atlantis*).

### ❤️ Assassin's Creed Origins (2021)
Everyone said this was better than Odyssey and indeed it is. The side quests are way better and everything feels well designed.

### ❤️ Assassin's Creed Valhalla (2024)
It is immensely satisfying to have an overpowered assassin + archer build and take down an entire camp silently.

### ❤️ Far Cry 5 (2019)
Probably my favourite game on the console. Amazing open world, supports a lot of play styles, nice co-op AI partners.

### ❤️ Rise of the Tomb Raider (2019)
Probably my favourite series. I love how *vertical* this whole game is.

### Shadow of the Tomb Raider (2019)
Fun game, great visuals and stealth sections.

### Far Cry 4 (2019)
Almost as good as 5. Better story as well.

### Watch Dogs (2019) & Watch Dogs 2 (2020)
WD2 has a beautiful open world and a satirical take on Silicon Valley. Stealth gameplay can be annoying but it shines when played aggressively.

### Watch Dogs: Legion (2025)
Enjoyable, but probably the weakest of the trilogy. The idea of recruiting anyone is fun at first but didn't make a huge difference over time.

### Batman: Arkham Knight (2019)
Disappointing. Too much driving.

### Batman: The Telltale Series & The Enemy Within (2022)
Fun games with some nice detecting additions. The *Shadows Edition* is beautiful.

### Ryse: Son of Rome (2021)
A lovely, violent game. Perfectly sized and mixed mechanics well.

### Ghostwire: Tokyo (2023)
The side quests are pretty well made and there is always something right around the corner that keeps you playing.
