# :material-chip:{ .anim-rotate } Sci-Fi & Cyberpunk

Futuristic worlds, neon lights, and high-tech exploration.

### ❤️ Cyberpunk 2077 (2025)
Night City just brims with personality! Focused on a melee + tech build with mantis blades. The 2.0 upgrade makes it a masterpiece.

### ❤️ Starfield (2023)
Lost myself into this game for a few months. Loved exploring, hoarding, and the main faction missions. I feel like I barely scratched the surface.

### Cloudpunk (2025)
A pleasant surprise. Lovely voxel-art style and well-told stories. It feels quite pleasant to drive a flying cab around doing nothing.

### Anno: Mutationem (2026)
A 2.5D side scroller with a lovely cyberpunk/pixel-art aesthetic. A solid mix of combat and exploration.

### Quantum Break (2021)
A game based on time-travel with alternating filmed episodes. A lot of fun!

### Control (2024)
A lovely game with an excellent art style and atmosphere. I had to tone down the difficulty to enjoy the flow.

### Soma (2022)
Lovely atmosphere and a good sci-fi plot. The exploration bits were really well done.

### Deus Ex: Mankind Divided (2019)
Badly wanted to like this, but struggled with the gameplay. Loved the world and character design.

### Mass Effect: Andromeda (2019)
Phenomenal world designs. Loved exploring each planet in the early stages.
