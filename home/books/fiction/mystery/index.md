# 🕵️ Mystery & Thriller

Tales of suspense, crime, and intrigue.

### The Hunting Party, by Lucy Foley (2025)
A bunch of snobbish rich couples spend the New Year in a remote retreat and things fall apart as one of the guests is killed. Nice insight into the masks they wear.

### Sad Cypress & Murder on the Links, by Agatha Christie (2024)
Always an enjoyable read. Two quick mysteries from the queen of crime.

### Make Me, by Lee Child (2024)
My first Jack Reacher book. Enjoyable and moves at a good pace. Recommended as a light read.

### Our Man in Havana, by Graham Greene (2023)
An enjoyable satire of the incompetent people running the government's secret service.

### ❤️Homicide: A Year on the Killing Streets, by David Simon (2023)
Chronicles a year in the lives of a squad of homicide detectives. Interspersed with essays on the morgue and courts. Highly recommended.
